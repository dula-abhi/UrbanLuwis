URBAN LUWIS is a proudly Sri Lankan brand established in 2025 under Secret Group (Pvt) Ltd, dedicated to redefining fashion and fabric standards. As a dynamic and evolving sub-brand, Urban Luwis offers a comprehensive range of high-quality clothing for men, women, and children, alongside stylish accessories, all under the Urban Luwis Clothing label.

More than just fashion, Urban Luwis is also home to Urban Luwis Fabric a premium line of high-quality fabrics imported from trusted suppliers, ensuring top-notch quality for both local and international markets.

Our Sub Brands

Urban Luwis proudly operates under Secret Group (Pvt) Ltd, alongside several trusted and reputed brands. These sub-brands each specialize in unique areas of fashion and textiles, working together to uplift Sri Lankan quality to global standards.
