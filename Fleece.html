Fleece Fabric

Fleece is a soft, warm, and insulating knit fabric, typically made from polyester or polyester blends. It is created by brushing one or both sides of the fabric to create a plush, fuzzy texture that traps air and retains heat, making it ideal for cold-weather clothing. Fleece is lightweight, breathable, quick-drying, and easy to care for, which makes it especially popular in casual wear and activewear.
